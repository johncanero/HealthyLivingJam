Vegetables

1. Ampalaya

In English, Bitter Gourd is a vegetable that could be bitter, but when I eat it, I feel healthier and stronger to start my day.

2. Chop Seuy

This dish has a variety of vegetables and a few meat. It is delicious and super healthy! Check out the pictures at Chop Suey

3. Broccoli

Whenever I see a Broccoli, it is like a baby tree and as soon as the brocolli is in my plate, I would want to bite and eat it immediatly.

4. Laing

If there is Laing, there is a smile on my face because it very delicious. If there is now face to face class at school, I would like to the carinderias or canteen to find Laing.

5. Lumpiang Togue

Another favorite vegetable food in my list, Lumpiang Togue is a spring roll filled with Mung bean sprouts and carrots. I love spring rolls and they are 100% delicious for me.

6. Puso ng Saging

I love the soup here and how it was made. It is a Filipino dish wherein banana blossoms are cooked in coconut milk or coconut cream See pictures of it here: Puso ng Saging.

7. Tortang Talong

Of course I will never forget Tortang Talong, an eggplant omelet, a delicious food for me where I usually eat it at Breakfast or Lunch.

Fruits

1. Avocado

Avocados are a gamechanger when it comes to fruits. I am pretty sure because of its texture and the color. I just love them and it is so delicious!

2. Coconut (Buko)

"The coconut nut is a giant nut If you eat too much, you'll get very fat"
With the white juice and it is meat, I feel more energetic to do my work. Go Coconut!

3. Banana

Banana, Banana, Banana everywhere.
Everyday in my house, I always see a banana and saba at our dining table. It is one of the glorious food that was created. Yellow strong!

4. Mango

Hello Mango! You are usually sliced into 3. One at the top, one at the bottom and one at the center are blessed to us. You are sweet and delicious after I eata meal.

5. Watermelon

Watermelon or Pakwan. You are so delicious but I feel like it takes a long time to remove your seeds yet nevertheless you are so sweet!

6. Orange

You are my strength in Vitamin C. You are the one who keeps me feel young. Your colors, your taste and sweet blend of smell. Thank you Orange!
